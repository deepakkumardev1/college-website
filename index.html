About Our College

A distinguished institution of higher learning with a legacy of academic excellence, providing world-class education and fostering character development.

Our Heritage & Mission

Established in 1947, Shri Varshney College has been a beacon of knowledge and enlightenment for over seven decades. Our institution is committed to providing quality education that combines academic excellence with practical skills, fostering holistic development of our students.

We believe in nurturing critical thinking, creativity, and ethical values in our students. Our experienced faculty members ensure that every student receives personalized attention and guidance needed to achieve their full potential.

Admission Instructions

  • Check eligibility criteria for your desired program
  • Complete the online application form
  • Upload required documents (10th & 12th mark sheets, ID proof)
  • Pay the application fee online
  • Receive admission status via email
  • Complete online registration and fee payment
  • Collect admission letter and ID card from campus
